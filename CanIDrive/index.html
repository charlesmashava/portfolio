Can I Drive?

PWDs can apply for a driver's license in LTO

The LTO does allow PWDs to operate motorized vehicles in the Philippines, provided that they are equipped to do so, and able to do so while following the rules and regulations of the road.

Car Suggestions for PWDs

Is there a specific car for PWDs?

Not exactly. However, the different types of disabilities can definitely affect the type of vehicle a PWD can use. Some disabilities may also require a car to be modified to fit their capabilities properly.
